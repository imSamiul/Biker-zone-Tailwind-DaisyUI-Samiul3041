Ride-on R15 V4 with Smile

Yamaha's R15 V4 is a sporty motorcycle known for its aggressive looks and exciting performance. This iteration boasts a peppy, refined engine for thrilling rides and sharp handling for confident maneuvering. Build quality has also seen a jump, with premium finishes and a clean cockpit.
